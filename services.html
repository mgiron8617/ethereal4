Gleaming Fairy Flying

Create your own fairy wings! Experience a magical moment with our fairies as you fly around our gleaming fairy dust to balance your energy.

Astrology Art Therapy

Delve into your artistic work by exploring your natal chart with the help of our astro creatures. You will learn about your birth chart and piece together an understanding of your existence.

Mermaid Meditation

5 senses! Through our underwater subway, enter the oceanic kingdom to meet with our mesmerizing mermaids. Learn all about our 5 senses meditation practices with the use of ocean melodies, hypnotic spells, irresistible touch by sea creatures, and much more.

Space Self-Exploration

Explore and navigate our journey quest. Our quest guides you to face challenges at every stop in the universe with an ultimate prize awaiting your presence in the very end. Learn how to handle difficult situations in your life with the help of our starlight angels.

Mushroom Mindfulness

Connect with nature as you breathe in the illuminating dust under our mushrooms to eliminate bad energy spreading throughout your body. Our mushrooms provide clarity, wisdom, and mindfulness for a breathtaking magical experience.

Become friends and learn to craft with our 7 dwarfs! Craft wood creations as you help to build and expand our beauty that is our mystical realm. We are always looking to enhance our world for a better experience and with your ideas, it makes us value collaboration and teamwork.

Botanical Tea

Love tea? Our team of hornet fairies provide excellent teachings on how to create herb teas with honey and enchanting pixie dust. Create your very own delightful tea with your preference amount of sweetener to cleanse the soul.

Visual Storytelling Sails

Sail away in our canoe as you travel along inside our blaze cavern. Enjoy a pleasant visual story that depicts memories of your childhood for a realization of your purpose in world.

Crystal Chemist Creation

Be a scientist for a day! Play around and learn to create potions for medication. Use our healing crystals and book of ancient quartz medicine to create various forms of potions to help cure sickness.

Sit and relax as our ancient oak tree reads a fairytale of your choosing. Learn to become independent and escape the world for a short moment under the illuminating fairy lights.

Forest Fears Triumph

Our biggest fears are our worst enemies, therefore our team of forest dragons provide assistance to help tag team against your worst fear. Battle it out and live life with fearlessness.
